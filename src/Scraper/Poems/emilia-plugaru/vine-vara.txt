                        Vine vara, vine vara
                        Cu cireşe coapte, dulci,
                        Ploi albastre, flori şi fluturi
                        Şi fâneţe albe-n lunci.
 
                        Vine vara, vine vara!
                        Hai la munte, hai la mare!
                        La bunici hai, la cireşe!
                        Vine vara cu mult soare!
